Ikigai novel

The book focuses on the topics related to living better, living happy and living long. The authors construe ikigai and its rules. To understand it well, they conducted a total of one hundred interviews in Ogimi, Okinawa trying to unfold the longevity secrets of centenarians and supercentenarians.
